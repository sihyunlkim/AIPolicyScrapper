Use of this Guide

The AI resources provided on this guide are new and evolving.  We carefully curate the resources posted here, and try to avoid information that promotes the unethical use of generative AI.

Research-Related AI Tools

Some examples (not comprehensive!):

  • Elicit
    Analyzes research papers
  • Explain Paper
    Makes research papers "easy to read"
  • perplexity
    Searches for concise answers
  • Consensus
    Searches for scholarly research
  • Semantic Scholar
    Searches for scholarly research
  • lit maps
    Visualizes scholarly literature
  • Research Rabbit
    Visualizes scholarly literature
  • scite
    Analyzes citations *subscription only*
  • JSTOR (beta)
    Provides AI assistance within JSTOR database
  • jenni
    Described as a "research assistant"
  • Midjourney
    Creates visuals from descriptive prompts
