AAMU Part of Artificial Intelligence Partnership

December 08, 2022

Boosting Machine Learning

Alabama A&M University is part of the Artificial Intelligence Learning Alliance (AILA) facilitated by Georgia Tech and Meta AI, whose researchers are forming advanced courses “at the frontier” of research and development efforts.

AILA aims to provide curriculum support, cutting-edge education and to increase the representation of historically marginalized researchers in machine learning.

For additional information, contact Dr. Ed Pearson III, assistant professor, College of Engineering, Technology and Physical Sciences, at (256) 372-4480 or e-mail ed.pearson@aamu.edu.
