Research That Connects

At the Institute for Social Science Research (ISSR), we advance meaningful research that addresses society’s most pressing challenges.

WHO WE ARE

The ISSR is your partner in turning big ideas into real-world impact. Based at The University of Alabama, we help researchers design better studies, analyze data, and communicate findings that shape communities. Whether you’re looking to launch a survey, apply for a grant, or collaborate on a research project, we can assist you. ISSR can support you from idea to outcome.

Research That Drives Change

Gender, Stereotypes, and Career Pathways

Dr. Joan Barth’s research explores how gender stereotypes and early experiences shape career interests. This is carried out through multiple projects.

Eggonomics: Examining Ethics in Reproductive Health

Dr. Diane Tober’s research examines the egg donation industry, focusing on ethical, economic, and social factors influencing reproductive decision-making.

The TRIP Lab: Innovating Road Safety & Injury Prevention

ISSR’s Translational Research for Injury Prevention (TRIP) Lab researches distracted driving, post-concussion recovery, and AI in transportation safety.
