LEGENDARY ACADEMICS

Students are empowered to unlock their full potential with over 100 top-ranked majors and programs, accelerated advanced degree options and more.

MAJORS AND PROGRAMS

Students can explore 200+ majors and degree programs offered through UA’s 12 individual colleges and schools.

UA ONLINE

With dedicated support from enrollment to graduation, students have access to 100+ undergraduate, graduate, doctoral and certificate programs, all offered in a flexible, online format.

RESEARCH

Through 30 research institutes and centers across campus, UA is addressing some of the most pressing challenges facing society, while making a global difference.

EXPERIENTIAL LEARNING

Learning extends far beyond the classroom as students take part in real-world educational experiences. Starting in fall 2026, all UA undergraduate programs will include an Experiential Learning component.
