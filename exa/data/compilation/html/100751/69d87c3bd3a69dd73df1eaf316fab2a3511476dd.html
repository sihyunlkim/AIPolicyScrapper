AI Teaching Network

Welcome to The University of Alabama AI Teaching Network! This video library features short, practical clips of use cases and advice for responsibly using generative AI in teaching practices. Use the drop-down menu below to filter by category or explore the entire library for content that fits your teaching needs.

AI Teaching Network

  • AI and Copyright Law

    AI is reshaping intellectual property and copyright law conversations, tackling questions like who owns the rights to AI-created works. Dr. Lawrence Cappello discusses the legal and ethical challenges that educators must grapple with when using generative AI.

    Play Video

  • AI and Data Security Fundamentals

    This video highlights the critical importance of data security in the AI era. Dr. Lawrence Cappello explores the risks of data leakage, practical strategies for protecting student data, and the shared responsibility of instructors and students in safeguarding sensitive information.

    Play Video

  • AI and FERPA Compliance

    Get a quick and clear breakdown of FERPA, the federal law protecting student education records, and what it means for college instructors nationwide. This video offers valuable insights for anyone seeking to understand FERPA compliance and uphold student privacy.

    Play Video

  • AI and Student Privacy: A Primer

    Every student has a right to data privacy, but AI is creating new challenges. Dr. Lawrence Cappello explains why student privacy matters and how educators can protect their students’ data in the age of artificial intelligence.

    Play Video

  • AI and Writing Assignments

    In this video, Dr. Lawrence Cappello analyzes the benefits and challenges generative AI presents in writing assignments. While AI has the potential to transform how students approach college writing, proper guidance and assignment construction are essential to ensure students develop key writing skills.

    Play Video

  • AI as a Grading Assistant – Simplifying the Process

    Dr. Laura McNeill shares how generative AI can be used to assist with grading student writing assignments along with best practices to ensure AI tools are used responsibly.

    Play Video

  • AI for Aligning Objectives with Assessments

    Dr. Laura McNeill explains how educators can use generative AI to align student learning objectives with course assessments.

    Play Video

  • AI for Allegories and Metaphors

    Discover how AI can transform your teaching by helping you create powerful metaphors and allegories that make complex concepts unforgettable. Watch this video to see a practical AI prompt in action and learn how to save time while crafting stories that resonate with your students.

    Play Video

  • AI for Creating Learning Objectives Aligning with Outcomes

    Dr. Laura McNeill explains how educators can use generative AI to align course objectives with student learning outcomes.

    Play Video

  • AI for Helping Students Revise Writing Assignments

    Dr. Laura McNeill outlines how instructors can use generative AI to aid students in the writing revision process with a sample prompt and best practices shared.

    Play Video

  • AI for Image Generation in Course Design

    Dr. Katie Chiou explains how to use AI for image generation and how AI-generated images offer new opportunities to engage students in critical thinking and creative projects across disciplines.

    Play Video

  • AI Image Generation: Techniques and Tools

    Dr. Katie Chiou eoutlines tools and approaches instructors can use to create custom visuals that engage students and enhance learning.

    Play Video
